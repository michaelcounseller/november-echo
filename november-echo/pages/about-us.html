Who Are We?

The Nameless was founded in 2018 by the Owner Matthew J. Konis (MaximusXCIII). He convinced his friends in the League Of Legends Community to help create a League of Legends Team Organization with Teams Randing from Iron all the way to the Challenger Scene. With much success over the past three years Maximus and his team have decide to expand and create a League for other Teams to compete in and also an Event Server to give back to the large League of Legends community.
